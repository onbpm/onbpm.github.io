BPM Business Process Management

Business Process Management Systems (BPMS) have become essential part of the daily work of most large and medium-sized companies. BPM Systems are especially suitable for companies engaged in the service sector, where there s a need to serve a large number of customers. Banks, telecoms, insurance, medical and government agencies are already using BPMS capabilities that improves service quality, reduce operating costs and makes process flow transparent, and thus increase the speed of processing.

We offer:

  • Requirements analysis and the preparation of a complete BPM solution.
  • Development of executable business process models in BPMN notation.
  • External systems ntegration (BRMS, CRM, ERP).
  • Development of auto-generated documentation (contracts, reports, etc.).
  • UI for Business Processes.
  • Mobile Buniness Process versions development.
